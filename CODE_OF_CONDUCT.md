# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors to follow a standard code of conduct to keep this community welcoming and productive.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Practice patience and assume good intent.
- Provide constructive feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, derogatory comments.
- Threats or doxxing.

## Enforcement

If you witness or are subject to unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers.
